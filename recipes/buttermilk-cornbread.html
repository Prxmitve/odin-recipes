Delicious desert to go along with any type of meal for a family event or even just to make for fun.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Grease an 8-inch square pan
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Remove from heat and stir in sugar. Quickly whisk in egss.
  3. Combine buttermilk and baking soda in a small bowl; whisk mixture into the skillet. Stir in cornmeal, flour, and salt until well-blended and only a few lumps remain. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, 30 to 40 minutes.
