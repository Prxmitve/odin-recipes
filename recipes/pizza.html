Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

For the pizza dough

  • 1 1/2 Cups (355 ml) warm water (105F-115F)
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
    • 3 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (omit if cooking pizza in a wood-fired pizza oven
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

For making the pizza and toppings

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Cornmeal (to help slide the pizza onto the pizza stone)
  • Tomato sauce (smooth or pureed)
  • Firm mozzarella cheese, grated
  • Fresh soft mozzarela cheese, seperated into small clumps
  • Fontina cheese, grated
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • Mushrooms, very thinly sliced if raw, otherwise first sauteed
  • Bell peppers, stems and seeds removed, very thinly sliced
  • Italian sausage, cooked ahead and crumbled
  • Sliced black olives
  • Chopped fresh basi
  • Baby argula, tossd in a little olive oil, added as pizza comes out of the oven
  • pesto
  • Pepperoni, thinly sliced
  • Onions, thinly sliced raw or caramelized
  • Ham, thinly sliced

Method

Making the Pizza Dough

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise

Preparing the Pizzas

  1. Preheadt the pizza stone (or pizza pan or backing sheet)
  2. Divide the dough into 2 balls
  3. Prep the toppings
  4. Flatten the dough ball, and stretch out into a round
  5. Brush the dough top with olive oil
  6. Sprinkle the pizza peel with cornmeal, put flattened dough on top
  7. Spread with tomao sauce and sprinkle with toppings
  8. Slide pizza into the oven
  9. Bake
